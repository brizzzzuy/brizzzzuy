## `$ whoami`

CS student from **Tashkent, Uzbekistan** 🇺🇿 who picked the phone Xiaomi forgot — the **13 Lite (codename `ziyi`)** — and keeps it on the newest HyperOS anyway.

- 🔧 Porting **HyperOS / MIUI** from CN donor builds (Xiaomi 13 · 12 Lite · 15) to `ziyi` since **April 2025**
- 📦 Every release ships **debloated, integrity-fixed, performance-tuned** — and gets daily-driven before anyone else touches it
- 🖱️ One-click Windows flashers with every build, so non-technical users never open a terminal
- 📲 Distribution + support through my Telegram channel **[Berloga](https://t.me/brezyck)** and **[brizz.uz](https://brizz.uz)**
- 🧠 Philosophy: *learn by shipping — pick a device nobody supports, make it work, give it to real users*

## `$ cat skills.txt`

- **Port bring-up** — firmware & vbmeta handling, overlay fixes, sensor/display debugging across three different donor devices
- **Camera stack** — full CamX bring-up per port; ported **Leica Camera 6 from the Xiaomi 15 Ultra** as a standalone universal APK
- **Play Integrity fixes** — banking apps and Google services passing on unlocked, modified devices
- **Root & NFC** — KernelSU Next / SukiSU Ultra integration, NFC bring-up on rooted builds
- **Debloat & performance engineering** — safe-list driven debloating, EROFS repacking with build-time SELinux labeling, boot-time perf tuning (I/O scheduler, LMK, TCP), custom TSK RAM profiles
- **Magisk modules** — signature-verification disabler, theme-reset permission fix
- **Cross-OEM archaeology** — attempted a ColorOS→ziyi port; it failed, and taught me more about Android's vendor/system split than any success did
